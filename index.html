I discovered my passion for technology and software development during my A-Level studies.


I followed this passion and landed a software engineering apprenticeship with the financial company Hargreaves Lansdown.


During my apprenticeship I widened my knowledge in many areas of software development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and Java.


This gave me the skills necessary to create a range of side projects which I worked on while I was not occupied with building software for the company.

I have a passion for fitness and have always enjoyed exercise.


I try to ensure I go to the gym 5x a week as well as other forms of activity.


I have been training in the martial art of TaeKwonDo for 14 years and have competed nationally and internationally.


During this time I have achieved 3rd degree black-belt and represented England in squad matches.


I have obtained many valuable skills and qualifications from training and teaching TaeKwonDo, including Junior instructor, knowledge in safeguarding and first aid, as well as experience in teaching students and young children with disabilities.
